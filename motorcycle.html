Growing up around motorcycles

Ever since I was a little kid I was around motorcycles. There is a race track in Elkhart Lake Wisconsin called Road America which has the AMA superbike races every summer. My parents would take me to the races every year since I was in a stroller and it is still a continued annual tradition.

My dad has always owned motorcycles since he was a kid so I was always around them and even riding on the back of them with my dad. It's one of the hobbies that me and him do together and we usually take a 3-4 day trip in the summer.
